A review of uncertainty in in situ measurements and data sets of sea-surface temperature

Based on Kennedy, J. J. (2014), A review of uncertainty in in situ measurements and data sets of sea surface temperature, Rev. Geophys., 52, 132, doi:10.1002/2013RG000434. A review can only provide a snapshot of the literature, but there may be an advantage in having a review that can be updated from time to time. The text of this review is under version control and the files can be found on github. As new papers and other resources become available, I will update the text.
